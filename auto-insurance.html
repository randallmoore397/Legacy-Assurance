WHOLE LIFE ASSURANCE

Life Insurance structured to assist families deal with the unexpected expenditures that come with the passing of a loved one. 


With Whole Life, there are dual premium payment options available.


Whole Life structures a payment to designated beneficiaries or legal heirs in the event that the insured passes away.

TERM ASSURANCE

Term Assurance,Like Whole Life, offers families a policy to assist with expenses when a loved one (the insured) passes away. 


Term Assurance however is only applicable for the specified agreed term.


The benefit expires when the agreed term is concluded.

MODIFIED ANTICIPATED ENDOWMENT

With a Modified Anticipated Endowment, the insured is covered (for the agreed sum insured). Under Modified Anticipated Endowments, policy holders are eligible for incremental percentage payouts of the sum insured during the tenor of the endowment, with the full sum assured amount being paid out over time (in the case of survival) or due to passing. 


This product is available for 15, 20 or 25 year terms.


Policyholder Loans are also available under this scheme.

ENDOWMENT ASSURANCE

With an Endowment Assurance, the insured is covered (for the agreed sum insured). 


Policy holders are eligible for the sum insured at the maturity date of the endowment, or payment is made to beneficiary or legal heirs. 

This product is available for 15, 20 or 25 year terms. Policyholder Loans are also available under this scheme.
